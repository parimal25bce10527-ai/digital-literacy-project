Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for College Students

Financial/UPI: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; your PIN is only required when you are sending a payment.

Financial/UPI: Always verify the receiver's UPI ID and registered name on your screen before authorizing any transaction.

Use unique, strong passwords for your college email, banking apps, and social media accounts.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all critical platforms, including your GitHub and primary email.

Avoid logging into personal banking or financial apps while connected to public, unsecured campus Wi-Fi networks.

Do not click on unverified links in SMS or WhatsApp messages claiming your bank account is blocked or a package is delayed.

Regularly update your laptop and smartphone operating systems to patch known security vulnerabilities.

Keep your social media profiles private and restrict who can see your personal contact information.
